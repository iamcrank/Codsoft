What WE DO

Marine Conservation Initiatives:

Implementing a variety of programs and projects aimed at preserving and restoring marine ecosystems, including coastal cleanups, habitat restoration efforts, and marine protected area establishment.

Sustainable Fisheries Practices:

Advocating for and promoting sustainable fishing practices to prevent overfishing, reduce bycatch, and protect vulnerable marine species. This may involve supporting responsible fishing techniques, promoting seafood certifications, and working with fishing communities to implement sustainable management strategies.

Plastic Pollution Prevention:

Taking proactive measures to combat plastic pollution in oceans through awareness campaigns, policy advocacy, and community engagement. This may include initiatives to reduce single-use plastics, promote recycling and waste management practices, and support research and innovation in plastic alternatives.
